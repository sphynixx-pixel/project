    • About This Website

    Remembering Little Things is a small photographic portfolio created to celebrate the details that stick with us. The site collects a handful of favorite images — moments that felt meaningful when I pressed the shutter. Photography is a way of feeling, touching, and of loving; these images are my memory-keepers.

    The site is built with semantic HTML and a custom stylesheet. Images link to larger versions hosted by Unsplash (used here as placeholders). The design emphasizes large imagery, clear typography, and a calm color palette.
